Creating a scatter plot using d3

Via this scatter plot you gain a general idea about the correlation between percentage of female researchers and consumer confidence for six countries.
More precisely for the countries: France, Germany, Korea, the Netherlands, Portugal and the United Kingdom. The time period is 2007 - 2015. Logically
the percentage of female researchers is based on the total headcount of researchers in a country. For consumer confidence a number below 100 represents
a pessimistic outlook on the future economic situation which may result in more saving and less consuming and vice versa for a number above 100. Since
you can basically draw an imaginary straight line (without a slope) through each scatter plot I assume there isn't any correlation between the two variables
However, adding a regression line and calculating R squared would better support this assumption than just eyeballing the graphs. The general basis of how
this scatter plot is build using d3 v5 and javascript is explained in these online examples: Scatter plot and Interactivity
